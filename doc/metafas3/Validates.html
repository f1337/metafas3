It is IMPOSSIBLE to validate email addresses with regex and comply with most recent RFCs. Cal Henderson and Dominic Sayers have provided free PHP algorithms for testing email address validity against RFC3696. I (mf) prefer the readability of Cal's method, but either may be translated to AS3.
